Overview

The map contains a bubble shaded with the political party of everyone within the current radius of the circle. To change the size of the bubble scroll up and down the list of people. The people are sorted by how close they are to the given address and their age is displayed next to their first name and last initial. To see more information about a person, click on them. To see information about the current bubble, check the section titled "Current Stats".
